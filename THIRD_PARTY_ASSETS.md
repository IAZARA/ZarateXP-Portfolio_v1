# Third-party asset notes

The `assets/images/hd-icons/minesweeper.svg` and `assets/images/hd-icons/pinball.svg`
icons are original ZarateXP compositions. Their basic game-object geometry was informed by
the following CC0 references from SVG Repo:

- Minesweeper: https://www.svgrepo.com/svg/517849/minesweeper
- Pinball: https://www.svgrepo.com/svg/57845/pinball

The final icons were redrawn with original gradients, composition, lighting, bevels and
Windows XP-inspired colors. They do not embed Microsoft product icons or Windows game
resources.
